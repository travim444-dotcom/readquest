📖 READING COMPREHENSION

Mainline

Read, understand, and conquer — master all four subjects to earn your certificate!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

1

Click Start Game. The game has 60 questions (20 paragraphs × 3 questions each) across 4 subjects in fixed order.

2

Each subject has 15 questions (5 paragraphs). Complete each subject fully before moving to the next.

3

Read each paragraph carefully, then click "Done Reading" to start the 120-second answer timer. The paragraph auto-advances after 50 seconds if you don't click. You cannot go back!

4

🏆 The Certificate is only awarded for a Perfect Score — 60/60 across all subjects. Aim for 100%!
